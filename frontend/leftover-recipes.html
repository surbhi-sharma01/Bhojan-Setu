Make More from Leftovers

Simple, tasty recipes that turn leftover rice, bread, veg or rotis into new meals.

Vegetable Fried Rice

Uses: leftover cooked rice + mixed vegetables

  • Leftover cooked rice
  • Mixed veggies (carrot, peas, capsicum)
  • Soy sauce, green onion, oil
  1. Heat oil, sauté veggies until tender.
  2. Add rice, a splash of soy sauce and toss on high heat.
  3. Finish with chopped green onion and pepper. Serve hot.

Masala Bread Upma

Uses: stale bread

  • Stale bread cubes
  • Onion, mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric
  • Chopped tomato & coriander
  1. Temper mustard seeds, sauté onion, curry leaves and tomato.
  2. Add turmeric, salt and bread cubes; toss until coated.
  3. Garnish with coriander and a squeeze of lemon.

Roti/Paratha Chaat

Uses: leftover rotis / parathas

  • Leftover rotis (cut into strips)
  • Yogurt, chutney, sev, chopped onion & tomato
  1. Warm roti strips in a pan until slightly crisp.
  2. Layer with yogurt, tamarind/chili chutney, onion & sev.
  3. Sprinkle chaat masala and serve immediately.

Leftover Veg Frittata (Indian style)

Uses: leftover boiled/roasted vegetables

  • 3–4 eggs (or chickpea flour mix for vegan)
  • Chopped leftover vegetables
  • Salt, pepper, turmeric
  1. Beat eggs with spices, fold in vegetables.
  2. Pour into a non-stick pan, cook on low until set; finish under a grill if available.
  3. Slice and serve with chutney or bread.

Khichdi Remix

Uses: leftover rice, dal or cooked vegetables

  • Leftover rice or dal
  • Ghee/ oil, cumin, asafoetida, mixed spices
  • Optional: roasted nuts or sautéed vegetables
  1. Heat ghee, temper cumin and asafoetida.
  2. Add leftovers, a little water to loosen, simmer and season.
  3. Top with ghee and serve with pickle or yogurt.

Paneer/Tofu Stir-up

Uses: leftover paneer/tofu + veg

  • Leftover paneer or tofu
  • Sliced onion, bell pepper, leftover curry masala
  1. Sauté onion & pepper; add paneer/tofu and a spoon of leftover curry.
  2. Cook until heated through and slightly crisp.
  3. Serve with rotis or rice.

Tip: label small portions, refrigerate promptly, and always reheat evenly before serving.
